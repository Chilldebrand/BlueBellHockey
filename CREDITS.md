# Credits

## Character models (optional GLB art)

The intended rigged humanoid models follow the KayKit "Adventurers / Skeletons" convention
(animation clips `Idle`, `Walking_A`, `Running_A`, `1H_Melee_Attack_*`, `Death_A`), as used by
[levy-street/world-of-claudecraft](https://github.com/levy-street/world-of-claudecraft)
under `public/models/chars/`. Those assets are distributed under CC0 / MIT — preserve their
original license notices when copying the `.glb` files into
`packages/client/public/models/chars/`.

Until GLBs are added, the client renders tinted procedural skaters (no external assets).
